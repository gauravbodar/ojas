# Go-To-Market Strategy
**Agent:** Growth Hacker  
**Phase:** 1 — Strategy  
**Project:** Charaka Saṁhitā Ayurveda Empire  
**Date:** March 2026

---

## GTM Philosophy

We do not launch a product. We launch a movement.

The GTM strategy for Charaka is education-led → community-built → product-sustained. Every acquisition channel leads to content. Every content piece leads to the quiz. Every quiz leads to the email sequence. Every email sequence leads to a purchase.

**Core GTM loop:**

```
Free content → Prakriti quiz → Email nurture → First purchase
     ↑                                                ↓
Community ←────────────────── Referral ←─────────────┘
```

---

## Phase A GTM (Months 1–3): Seeding

**Goal:** 1,000 email subscribers. Zero ad spend. Pure content + community.

### Channel 1 — SEO Content (Primary Organic)
Target keywords that are high-intent and low-competition:
- "Ayurvedic morning routine for beginners" (3.2K/mo, low comp)
- "What is Vata dosha" (8.1K/mo, medium comp)
- "Dinacharya daily routine" (1.4K/mo, low comp)
- "Charaka Samhita explained" (900/mo, very low comp)
- "Pitta body type diet" (4.5K/mo, low comp)
- "Ashwagandha for stress dosage" (12K/mo, medium comp — avoid health claims)

**Strategy:** Publish 2 cornerstone articles per week. Every article ends with the Prakriti quiz CTA. Target 24 articles in 90 days.

---

### Channel 2 — Instagram / Short-form Video
**Content pillars for Reels / TikTok:**
1. "Charaka said this 2,000 years ago, and it explains everything about [modern problem]"
2. "Your dosha type and [sleep / food / exercise / stress]"
3. "The ancient Indian concept that changed how I think about [routine / food / health]"
4. "3 things the Charaka Saṁhitā says about [digestion / skin / energy]"

**Posting frequency:** 4 Reels/week, 5 Stories/week  
**Goal Month 3:** 2,000 followers, 200 quiz completions from this channel

---

### Channel 3 — Reddit (r/Ayurveda, r/HealthyLiving, r/nutrition)
**Approach:** Genuine contribution, not marketing.
- Answer questions with depth, cite Charaka
- Post original research threads ("I spent a month following Dinacharya. Here's what happened.")
- Link to content naturally, never as an ad

---

### Channel 4 — Podcast Guest Strategy
Target shows where our personas already listen:
- **Priya:** The Diary of a CEO, Feel Better Live More, Huberman Lab (adjacent)
- **Arjun:** Acquired, Lex Fridman (for credibility angle), diaspora-focused shows
- **James:** Tim Ferriss, The Knowledge Project, My First Million

**Pitch angle:** "The 2,000-year-old productivity system Silicon Valley hasn't discovered yet"  
**Goal:** 3 podcast appearances in first 6 months

---

### Channel 5 — LinkedIn (For B2B + Arjun Persona)
- Founder posts: 3×/week on the intersection of ancient philosophy + modern performance
- Article format: "What the Charaka Saṁhitā teaches about productivity [and why your biohacking stack misses the point]"
- Build toward B2B inquiries: "I help companies build Charaka-inspired wellness programs"

## Phase B GTM (Months 4–6): Converting

**Goal:** First 100 paying customers. $10K MRR.

### Launch Sequence — Dinacharya Kit

**Week 1:** Email to list — "Something we've been building for you"  
**Week 2:** Behind-the-scenes content — ingredient sourcing, formulation story  
**Week 3:** Founding Member offer — first 100 orders at 20% off + personal thank you note  
**Week 4:** Full public launch

**Launch KPIs:**
- 100 units sold in first 30 days
- Email open rate > 35%
- Conversion rate from list: > 5%

---

### Course Launch Sequence — Charaka Foundations

**Pre-launch (2 weeks):** Free webinar — "Introduction to Charaka: The Complete System for Modern Living"  
**Launch week:** Email sequence (4 emails over 5 days)  
**Evergreen:** Course enters email funnel permanently at Day 14 of nurture sequence

**Pricing:** $197 AUD / $127 USD (introductory), rising to $297 / $197

---

## Phase C GTM (Months 7–12): Scaling

**Goal:** $50K MRR. Community of 5,000. B2B pipeline open.

### Paid Acquisition (Start Month 7+)
Only turn on ads once the organic → quiz → email funnel is proven (conversion rate > 3% email → purchase).

**Platform priority:**
1. Meta (Instagram / Facebook) — visual, lifestyle, quiz-focused
2. Google Search — high-intent keyword targeting ("Ayurvedic routine," "dosha quiz")
3. YouTube pre-roll — educational content teasers

**Target CAC:** < $40 AUD for product customer, < $30 AUD for email subscriber  
**Target LTV:** > $400 AUD (Year 1: kit + course + subscription box × 2)

---

### Referral Program
- Every customer gets a unique referral link
- Referral reward: $20 credit for referrer + $15 discount for new customer
- Activate at first purchase (post-delivery email)

**Mechanism:** Charaka calls this concept "Sangha" — the healing power of community. Frame referrals as building your Sangha.

---

### Community-Led Growth
- Monthly free community workshop (Discord/Circle)
- "Charaka Challenge" — 21-day Dinacharya habit challenge, live cohort
- User-generated content: #MyCharakaMorning on Instagram
- Testimonials systematically collected at Day 30 and Day 90 post-purchase

## What We Will NOT Do

- Launch with paid ads before organic is proven
- Partner with influencers who don't understand Ayurveda
- Rush product launches before educational layer is built
- Make health claims under competitive pressure
- Discount heavily — it destroys premium positioning
