## SCQA Framework Summary

### Situation
The global Ayurveda market is a $20.4B category growing at 19.7% CAGR, projected to reach $85.8B by 2033. Digital-first D2C channels account for 41% of distribution and are the fastest-growing segment. Consumer demand for philosophy-backed, personalised wellness is accelerating post-pandemic.

### Complication
No premium, philosophy-first Ayurveda brand exists at scale in Western markets. Competitors either own the mass market (Patanjali), the functional beauty market (Mamaearth/WOW), or the product-only D2C space (The Ayurveda Experience). The Charaka Saṁhitā — the foundational text of Ayurveda — is unclaimed as a modern brand anchor. The 24-month window to establish category ownership is open now.

### Question
How do we build a scalable Ayurvedic empire anchored to the Charaka Saṁhitā, without crossing into medical advice, with a clear path to $150K MRR by month 18?

### Answer
A philosophy-first, education-led, personalization-powered empire with six revenue verticals: Knowledge Platform, D2C Products, AI Dosha Engine, Functional Food, Retreats/Events, and B2B White-label. The brand is built on authority (Charaka as the credential), community (learners before buyers), and compounding flywheels (content → quiz → email → purchase → referral → community).
